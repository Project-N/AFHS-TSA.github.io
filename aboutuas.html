Unmanned Aerial Systems (UAS)

Sometimes reffered to as a drone, an UAS is a flight instrument that is capable of flying without a human being on board.

Commercial

UAS has many commercial uses, not all of which are evident. One of the most common uses that have been researched is using UAS's to deliver packages. Amazon is currently researching this technology as well as other large businesses. Another use of UAS's is for filming. Footage can be captured from angles that were not previously possible to film. Some television channels have even started broadcasting sporting event footage from drones.

Agriculture Uses

UAS's can be used to monitor crops during the growing season. They can be used to analyse acres of crops faster than humans and can see problems that cannot be seen with the human eye through NDVI. These same drones can also inspect irrigation equipment, find weeds, find out how much fertilizer certain crops need, and monitor herds of cattle.
