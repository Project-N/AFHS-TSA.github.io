AFHS TSA Club

The AFHS TSA Club is open to all those who are interested in technology and like to compete. The club submits entries into the annual official TSA competition, talks about technology, and is just a great place for anyone who enjoys technology to hang out (A fair amount of Halo may be played in the process).
